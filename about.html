VIT

University was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with international standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis. The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students from all corners of the globe. All professors are learned and experienced in their field and are strongly encouraged to nurture the students according to their requirements. The global standards set at VIT in the field of teaching and research spur us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. Our Memoranda of Understanding with various international universities are one of our major strength. They provide for an exchange of students and faculty and encourage joint research projects for the mutual benefit of these universities. Many of our students, who pursue their research projects in foreign universities, bring high caliber to their work and esteem to India and have done us proud. With steady steps, we continue our march forward. We look forward to meeting you here at VIT.

SAS, VIT

The School of Advanced Sciences (SAS) includes Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics departments. The school offers M.Sc., M.Phil. and Ph.D. programmes in these disciplines. The faculty of the school comprises qualified and goal-oriented members whose research expertise include major frontier areas in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Currently, the faculty strength of the school is 186. In addition, the school benefits from the administration of 30 non-teaching personnel. The curriculum keeps abreast of the latest advancements in the respective domains and endeavors to develop scientists of high quality. Graduates from the school are much desired by companies in India and many have secured admissions in prestigious institutions and companies abroad.


Department of Chemistry

The Division of Chemistry at VIT Vellore apart from indulging in teaching basic engineering chemistry for the undergraduates is actively involved in research in various fields which are interdisciplinary and of social and technological importance. The significance of the expertise of the faculty members in diverse fields of Chemistry is given below. The faculty members have post-doctoral experience from various reputed universities both in abroad (US, UK, Israel, Germany) and India. Some of them have been awarded prestigious fellowships/awards such as Marie Curie Fellowship (European Union), FAPESP (Brazil), DS Kothari (UGC, India), CSIR pool scientist, best thesis award. Most of the faculty members have the reputation of being invited speakers at various conferences.

SIGMA XI-VIT

SIGMA XI is a research-based student-driven chapter of VIT UNIVERSITY which encourages students to design and innovate in all engineering fields. The institution is home to the most creative minds of the university, working on projects which have received global acclaim and recognition. SIGMA XI aims to provide technical growth to all its members and assists them to write and publish research papers. With an international presence, the chapter has been able to capture the essence of innovation, creativity, and reform towards research. SIGMA XI has the vision to harness technology and accelerate innovation in learning and inspire learners to reach their greatest potential. Moreover, the community has a broad mission to empower learners to flourish in a connected world by cultivating a passionate professional learning community.
